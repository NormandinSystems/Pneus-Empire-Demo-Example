Pollution Exists Inside, Too

Road travel is an integral part of our daily lives. However, dense traffic and urban roads emit air pollutants that can have a harmful impact on our health.

Those same outdoor pollutants slip right into your vehicle whenever you turn on the AC or heating. In fact, due to urban traffic, the air inside your cabin can be up to 6 times more polluted than the air outside!

"At Pneus Empire, we care about the air inside your vehicle. That's why we bring the exact filter you need and replace it on the spot, so you never have to wait at a repair shop."

Benefits of a Clean Filter

The cabin filter is the heart of your car's HVAC system. A well-kept filter ensures:

  • Better Air Quality

    Intercepts pollen, dust, and harmful allergens.

  • Efficient Defrosting

    Restores full airflow to clear your windshield faster in winter.

  • Maximum AC Performance

    Takes the strain off your blower motor, prolonging its life.

  • Odor & Condensation Control

    Controls humidity for better visibility and removes musty smells.

When should I replace it?

It is generally recommended to change the filter every 25,000 to 30,000 kilometres. However, heavy urban traffic and dusty roads can clog your filter much faster.

The more the filter is polluted, the more restricted the air entering your vehicle becomes, impacting both air quality and ventilation efficiency.

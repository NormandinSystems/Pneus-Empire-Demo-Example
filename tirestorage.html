Regulations in Canada

In Canada, it's important to adhere to regulations regarding tire storage to avoid fines. Storing tires on balconies or in other unsuitable locations can result in penalties. Ensure that tires are kept in a location that meets legal requirements and does not impact public safety or aesthetics.

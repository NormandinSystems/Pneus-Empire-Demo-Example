Mag/Rim Repair & Loaner Program

Don't get stranded by a cracked rim. We manage the entire process from the road to the fully repaired wheel.

How The Loaner Program Works

  1. Mobile Assessment: We come to your location to inspect the cracked or damaged rim.
  2. Loaner Installation: We install a temporary loaner tire/rim so you can keep driving.
  3. Rim Repair: Your broken rim is sent to our specialized repair facility.
  4. Return & Swap: Once fixed, we return to install your original repaired rim and take back the loaner.

The Science of Clear Vision

This glass rain repellent treatment creates an invisible protective layer on the glass that helps water move away more quickly.

It can improve visibility by up to 34% when driving in rain or other challenging weather conditions. By reducing the buildup of water, snow, and ice, it also helps improve wiper performance.

"Why wait at a service centre? We apply this premium treatment right in your driveway. The perfect add-on during your tire change!"

The Numerous Benefits

  • Substantially Increases Visibility

  • Reduces Glare

    Minimizes light reflection in rain and increases night visibility.

  • Eases Ice & Snow Removal
